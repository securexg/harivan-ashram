# Harivan Ashram Website Plan

## Project Overview
A comprehensive website for Harivan Ashram, a nature-oriented spiritual retreat center under The Art of Living in Chandigarh.

## Website Structure & Navigation

### Primary Navigation
- **Home**: Hero section with welcoming message and key information
- **About**: Harivan Ashram story, connection to Art of Living, and Gurudev's vision
- **Events**: Past and upcoming activities, celebrations, and community programs
- **Courses**: YLTP and other educational programs
- **Gallery**: Visual content from events and daily ashram life
- **Contact**: Location, contact details, and visitor information

## Key Features Implemented

### 1. Hero Section
- Welcoming headline with floating animation
