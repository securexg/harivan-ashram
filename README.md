# Harivan Ashram Website

A nature-oriented spiritual retreat center under The Art of Living, located in Chandigarh.

## Project Overview

Harivan Ashram is a new ashram of The Art of Living focused on nature-based spiritual growth and community service. The website serves as a primary landing page to:

- Introduce Harivan Ashram to visitors
- Showcase connection to The Art of Living and Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar
- Display events, courses, and activities
- Provide contact and location information
- Share photos and gallery content

## Key Features

- **Hero Section**: Welcoming introduction with nature imagery
- **About Section**: Connection to Art of Living and Gurudev
- **Events Section**: Past and upcoming activities
- **Courses Section**: YLTP and other programs
- **Gallery Section**: Photos from events and daily activities
- **Contact Section**: Location, email, and phone details

## Technologies Used

- HTML5
- Tailwind CSS
- Vanilla JavaScript
- Responsive Design

## Target Audience

- Spiritual seekers
- Art of Living members
- Local community members
- Youth interested in YLTP programs
- Visitors looking for spiritual retreats
